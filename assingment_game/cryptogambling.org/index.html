Key Ideologies

We are paving the way towards the future of online gambling by raising awareness about the issues and solutions regarding trust. Supporting the industry with education material and development tools, we are promoting essential practices that should be standard across the industry to provide a completely honest and fair gambling experience.

Demand for a Trustless Environment

There is a serious need to lower the trust required between players and casino operators. One of the biggest concerns that plagues the industry is the distrust that has evolved from unreliable auditing processes. Provably fair algorithms solve this issue by providing completely transparent and verifiable ways to facilitate the generation of true randomness. From rolling a dice to picking a card from a deck, provably fair algorithms guarantee that all actions conducted during a game are completely legitimate, genuine and available for everyone to audit.

If you would like to learn more about the technical workings of provably fair algorithms, you can download the whitepaper.

Provide a fair experience

We want to encourage the growth of the industry by supporting better practices. We believe in treating users with respect and decency, and we want to promote those who embrace that approach.

Complete protection of privacy

Identity protection and privacy is of the highest concern within our evolving online world. We firmly believe that everyone has the right to complete security and privacy of information. We do not want to endorse any entity that neglects the importance of keeping their data safe and secure.

Having a flawless reputation

Reputation is something that is heavily scrutinised over in the online gaming industry. We want to promote those who are dedicated to not only building a strong reputation, but also developing strategies to maintain and potentially improve on current practices.

Advocate responsible gambling

We want to prioritise the well-being of all players. Gambling should provide a form of entertainment without exploitation. We encourage the development and promotion of responsible gambling initiatives to provide support for those who need it.

Verified Operators

The Crypto Gambling Foundation prides itself in the verification and promotion of genuine true fair gambling operators. Through our processes and strict ideologies, players can know that the operators are running a completely transparent gambling experiences, and operators can not only evaluate their operators and coding, but also benchmark their practices with those industry leaders who are paving the way for the future of online gambling.

To be a verified operator by us, means that we have evaluated the provably fair algorithm built into these providers platform to be true and fair. As a player, it’s always best practice practice to also test and verify the fairness of your bets regularly to ensure further integrity checks.

About Us

What is CGF?

Bitcoin gambling has been revolutionary to the gambling industry on so many levels. With low barriers of entry this has enabled people to create new & successful games which have changed the scene.

Most importantly however Bitcoin gambling brought along the concept of true fairness also known as provably fair. Before this mean of ensuring security of players, players had to trust that casinos & gambling software providers were not manipulating outcomes.

With this approach casino owners have created a tainted image for online gaming. Thanks to some early adopters of this new approach to online gambling we started to see a shift in ideology. 2013 brought along positive press of this change and the concept of this newly found fairness.

Mission & Vision Statement

We believe that gambling should be fair and transparent for all involved parties! The only way to ensure this is using provably fair algorithms thus removing the need to trust a third, and sometimes unknown, source. Provably fair is today the only way ensure true fairness and trust making it impossible to manipulate bets and outcomes.

The CGF’s vision is that the gambling industry is 100% fair and transparent. Only by creating a completely fair and transparent arena you’re able to mitigate all issues of trust. We will work diligently with all our members to promote fairness, transparency and trust within crypto gambling.
